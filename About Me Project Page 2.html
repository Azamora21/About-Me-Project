Aspirations

The main difference between aspirations and dreams are the reality concerning them both.

Dreams are simply someones view of what their role would be in a "perfect world."

On the other hand someones aspirations are the tangible hope and ambition that they display to achieve their desired goal.

So with that defined I have perfect world dreams but my aspirations are sperate than them. I aspire to become a scientist, to be more specific, an astrophyiscist.


Gained Aspiration

My love for the celestial bodies above us right now didnt come out of the blue but was found in the little moments.

What sparked my love for the stars began on night on a cruise ship. My family was out on the balcony lookink at the ocean and listening to the calm waters.

I instead was captivated from what I saw in the water. What seemed to be counteless of twinkling spots on the water entranced me.

I looked up from the waters to see vast stretches of stars coating the skies, ever since i have done nothing but try and further my knmowledge of the heavens above us.
